What is Dionysus?
Dionysus is a recommendation engine for beer, wine, and whisky.

Tell us what you like and we'll suggest other drinks you'll love using the Alcohol Genome Project's 10,000+ drink flavor profiles. We've analyzed nearly 1 million drink reviews to give you recommendations customized to your personal tastes that you can count on.

Advertising with Dionysus

For breweries, wineries, and distilleries, Dionysus offers uniquely taste-targeted Sponsored Recommendations. We won't waste your money showing ads to people who will not actually buy what you are selling, we will only show the Sponsored Recommendations to people who like drinks with similar flavor profiles.

If you are interested in advertising with us, email dionysus@alcoholgenome.com
