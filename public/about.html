Love what you drink.

The Alcohol Genome Project asks you what beers you like and recommends new ones you'll love.

How many times have you walked into a bar or liquor store and despite (or because of) the many options, had no idea what to get? Well friends and fellow drinkers, search no longer! The Alcohol Genome Project has analyzed more than 500,000 reviews to develop unique drink profiles and give you personalized recommendations based on your tastes.

We're also working to bring you wine and whisky recommendations, so stay tuned.
Life's too short for bad beer, wine, and whisky. Love what you drink!
